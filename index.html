This is the official FIFA World Cup 26™ Sales Agents directory, powered by On Location — the sole Official Hospitality Provider appointed by FIFA for the FIFA World Cup 26™. Every company listed on this platform has been reviewed, vetted, and officially appointed by On Location to sell official, ticket-inclusive hospitality packages for the tournament.

Our Authority & Appointment

On Location holds the exclusive mandate from FIFA as the Official Hospitality Provider of the FIFA World Cup 26™. Under this mandate, On Location has appointed a select global network of Sales Agents to represent the Official Hospitality Programme across key markets worldwide. This directory lists only those agents who hold a valid appointment document issued by On Location, carry an official agent reference number, and have agreed to operate within FIFA and On Location's strict compliance and consumer protection standards.

How Agents Are Verified

All agents listed in this directory have gone through a formal appointment process that includes:

  • Submission and review of company credentials, legal documentation, and financial standing
  • Signing of an official Sales Agent Agreement with On Location
  • Issuance of a unique Official Agent Reference Number

Your Protection as a Consumer

Purchasing through an agent listed on this platform gives you genuine, FIFA-issued match tickets, transparent pricing in line with official programme guidelines, access to premium hospitality suites and exclusive experiences at host venues, and professional handling of travel logistics, visa support, and accommodation. If you encounter an agent claiming to sell FIFA World Cup 26™ hospitality who does not appear in this directory, we strongly encourage you to verify their credentials before making any payment.
